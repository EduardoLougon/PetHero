# PetHero - Brand Image Guidelines

## Brand Core

PetHero is a superhero-inspired pet experience center located in São Francisco, Niterói (RJ), Brazil.

Main Statement:
"Nossa missão é salvar o seu dia."

The brand represents:
- Freedom
- Protection
- Emotional relief
- Professional care
- Well-being
- Trust

This is not a pet storage service.
This is a liberation-based care experience.

---

## Core Brand Pillars

1. Freedom (Livre de gaiolas)
2. Low Stress handling
3. Emotional safety
4. Therapeutic care
5. Functional nutrition
6. Premium hospitality
7. Community engagement

Pets are not objects.
Pets are family.
The environment must visually reinforce this.

---

## Emotional Atmosphere

The design must communicate:

- Relief
- Safety
- Warmth
- Heroic protection
- Calm confidence

The superhero theme is strong but not chaotic.
It must feel structured and reliable.

---

## Color Palette

Primary: #F7E8CB  
Use for main backgrounds and soft sections.

Secondary: #003E52  
Use for headings, structural elements, and footer.

Accent: #FD1024  
Use only for CTAs, highlights, badges, and key emphasis.

The accent red represents:
Action.
Heroism.
Energy.
Urgency.

Do not overuse red.

---

## Visual Identity Enhancements

Because PetHero is cage-free, avoid visuals that imply:

- Confinement
- Grids
- Bars
- Heavy boxed layouts

Use:
- Open spacing
- Airy sections
- Rounded shapes
- Flowing layouts

The design should visually express freedom.

---

## Service-Specific Visual Nuances

Low Stress Bath:
- Calm visuals
- Soft lighting
- Relaxed pets

Ozone Bath:
- Clean, therapeutic, clinical but warm
- Subtle water or light effects

Hotel:
- Cozy
- Structured
- Premium hospitality

Snacks:
- Natural textures
- Ingredient highlights
- Functional benefit badges

Yoga com adoção:
- Community
- Warm sunlight
- Open environment

---

## Graphic Style

Include:

- Hero-style badges
- Power icons
- Dynamic section separators
- Clean comic-style typography

Avoid:
- Overloaded comic graphics
- Excessive cartoon bubbles
- Childish illustrations

Keep it premium-comic, not cartoonish.

---

## UI Feel

Buttons:
- Rounded
- Slight hover elevation
- Accent red
- Strong contrast

Cards:
- Soft shadow
- Clear hierarchy
- Subtle border
- Badge-style labeling

Animations:
- Smooth
- Confident
- Purposeful
- Not playful gimmicks
